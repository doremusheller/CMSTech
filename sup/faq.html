The basics.

What is S’up?

S’up is a hemp mocktail beverage made for alcohol-free social sipping. It is designed to offer cocktail-style flavor and a relaxed beverage experience without alcohol.

Does S’up contain alcohol?

No. S’up is an alcohol-free beverage.

What does “hemp mocktail” mean?

It means S’up is inspired by mocktail flavors and made with hemp-derived ingredients. It is intended for adults looking for a modern alternative to alcoholic drinks.

How should I drink S’up?

Serve chilled, pour over ice, or dress it up with citrus, herbs, fruit, or a cocktail garnish. It works straight from the can or as a base for alcohol-free mixed drinks.

Effects & experience.

How will S’up make me feel?

Everyone’s experience may vary. S’up is made for relaxed, social moments, but individual effects depend on your body, tolerance, serving size, and other factors.

How long does it take to feel anything?

Timing can vary by person. Start with one serving, give yourself time, and avoid consuming more until you understand how it feels for you.

Can I drive after drinking S’up?

Do not drive or operate machinery if you feel impaired. Use responsibly and follow all applicable laws.

Who should not drink S’up?

S’up is intended for adults. Do not consume if you are pregnant, nursing, underage, or have been advised by a medical professional to avoid hemp-derived products.

Start low. Sip slow. Let the drink do its very reasonable thing.
