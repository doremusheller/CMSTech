Signature Law Blog Platform Recommendation

Prepared for: Partners, Sumer Blackner

Two practical paths for publishing attorney-written legal content, improving search visibility, and keeping the machinery out of the way.

Option A

WordPress

WordPress is proven, flexible, and widely used. It can support a strong legal blog and handle every important SEO requirement.

It also comes with ongoing care. Plugins, updates, backups, security, and compatibility issues do not manage themselves.

Pros

  • Established and widely supported
  • Strong SEO capabilities
  • Familiar editor for many users
  • Easy to add features later
  • Large developer community
  • Many ready-made integrations

Cons

  • Regular software and plugin updates
  • Plugins can conflict or break
  • Security and backups need attention
  • More moving parts than the firm needs
  • Ongoing maintenance cost
  • Dependence on third-party plugins

Search visibility

SEO considerations

WordPress does not receive a ranking bonus from Google.

Google cares about the quality of the article, the authority of the author, the speed of the page, the structure of the site, and whether the content is easy to find and understand.

Both options can provide every important SEO feature:

  • Search-friendly page addresses
  • Automatic XML sitemaps
  • Article and FAQ structure
  • Attorney author pages
  • Canonical page references
  • Internal links to practice areas
  • Mobile-friendly layouts
  • Fast page loads
  • Clean, search-friendly HTML
  • Social sharing previews

The custom system may also have a practical SEO advantage. It can be built around Signature Law's exact practice areas, attorneys, locations, and internal linking strategy from day one.

That means less clutter, fewer technical dependencies, and a cleaner path between each article and the legal service page it supports.

The biggest SEO advantage will not come from WordPress or custom software. It will come from attorneys consistently publishing useful, accurate, authoritative legal content.

Build the custom AI-assisted publishing system.

For approximately 48 attorney-written articles each year, the custom system gives Signature Law the cleanest balance of SEO performance, cost control, simplicity, and long-term ownership.

No SEO sacrifice Every meaningful search feature can be built directly into the platform.
Less machinery No plugin stack, surprise updates, recurring licenses, or permanent housekeeping.

The custom system has no meaningful SEO disadvantage compared with WordPress. In several areas, it may be stronger because the platform can be tailored to Signature Law's content, attorneys, practice areas, and internal linking structure instead of being adapted through plugins.
