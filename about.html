Founder and company background

Built across education, enterprise, startup, and mission-driven work

Craig Schneider is a lifelong educator, technologist, and problem solver who began his career building infrastructure at Washington University in St. Louis. Across large corporate, startup, educational, and non-profit environments, he has focused on helping people use technology to do great things. That work has consistently centered on practical systems, clear teaching, and service to the people doing the work. CMS Technical Optimization brings those strengths together to help leaders and teams understand AI, apply it with confidence, and move from curiosity to measurable outcomes without losing sight of the human side of the work.

Principles and operating approach

Technology is a tool. People create the value.

Technology is a tool, like a chisel or a paintbrush. It is humans who do the interesting stuff with tools. CMS Tech Optimization is a service-focused, educationally centered organization with one aim: empowering teams and leaders with the training, understanding, and ongoing guidance they need to use AI well.

Principle

Tech exists to serve people. Not the other way around.

Focus

Understanding your people and how AI can help them succeed.

Outcome

Smart, targeted instruction with ROI in weeks, not months or years.
