Expanded Biography

Depth-tested counsel.

Don began his legal career in the high-pressure world of harbor disputes, salvage claims, and aggressively worded cease-and-desist letters written on waterproof stationery. He quickly earned a reputation for spotting weak arguments, grabbing them firmly, and not releasing them until the court reporter needed a break.

Clients value Don’s ability to manage multiple litigation fronts at once: drafting motions, reviewing contracts, marking exhibits, intimidating expert witnesses, and opening a jar of olives without assistance.
