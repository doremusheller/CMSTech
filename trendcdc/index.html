Technology in Service of Community Development

TREND CDC's work is centered on strengthening neighborhoods, supporting entrepreneurs, expanding economic opportunity, and creating lasting community impact throughout Chicago communities.

CMS Technical Optimization is helping explore how intelligent automation, digital creation, and mission-focused AI systems can support that work through improved operations, stronger organizational capacity, and scalable technology solutions.

Focus Areas

Integration & Automation

CMS Technical Optimization integrates artificial intelligence into existing business processes to automate repetitive tasks, streamline workflows, reduce administrative burden, and improve organizational efficiency.

The result is more time, greater organizational capacity, and more resources available for mission-critical work.

Digital Creation

Modern AI tools can rapidly create professional video, audio, graphics, presentations, outreach campaigns, educational content, training materials, and visual assets.

Complex ideas can be communicated quickly, consistently, and at scale while dramatically reducing production time.

Mission Consolidation

CMS Technical Optimization helps organizations gather data, documents, institutional knowledge, communications, operational systems, and digital assets from across organizational silos into a unified intelligent ecosystem.

By combining information sources and applying AI-driven analysis, leadership gains better visibility, stronger decision-making capabilities, and improved alignment around core organizational goals.
