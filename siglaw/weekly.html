Chief Digital Officer Tip of the Week

Memory Costs Are Moving Fast

The cost of memory-heavy PCs has gone mad due to a combination of global supply disruption and aggressive memory consumption by AI data centers. As AI farms continue buying up high-performance memory, laptop and desktop prices are likely to rise in the coming months.

Recommendation: If laptop or workstation orders are needed this year, earlier is better. Waiting may mean higher prices, weaker availability, or both.
