Expanded Biography

Transactions, disputes, and strategic growth.

Matthew A. Rivela is a Founding Partner at Signature Law Partners, PLLC. For more than twenty years, he has represented businesses, entrepreneurs, investors, executives, and high-profile individuals in complex transactional and litigation matters. His practice focuses on mergers and acquisitions, commercial litigation, corporate law, and strategic business counseling.

Throughout his career, Matthew has advised clients across a wide range of industries on acquisitions, divestitures, corporate governance, contract negotiations, financing transactions, and other significant business matters. He has structured, negotiated, and closed transactions with an aggregate value exceeding $3 billion, representing buyers, sellers, investors, and companies in matters ranging from middle-market transactions to complex strategic acquisitions.

His practical, business-focused approach enables clients to identify opportunities, manage risk, and maximize value.

In addition to his transactional practice, Matthew has extensive experience litigating complex commercial disputes across numerous state and federal jurisdictions throughout the United States, as well as in international matters.

He has represented clients in matters involving breach of contract claims, shareholder and partnership disputes, fiduciary duty claims, business torts, employment disputes, and restrictive covenant and anti-competition matters, including non-compete, non-solicitation, and trade secret litigation. His litigation experience provides a unique perspective when advising clients on transactions, allowing him to anticipate and mitigate risks before they become disputes.

As a Founding Partner of Signature Law Partners, Matthew leverages his extensive experience and expertise in complex transactions and high-stakes litigation to deliver sophisticated legal representation tailored to each client's objectives.

He serves as a trusted advisor to businesses, entrepreneurs, and investors, helping them navigate complex legal challenges, capitalize on opportunities, and achieve successful outcomes, while consistently delivering meaningful value and measurable results for his clients.
